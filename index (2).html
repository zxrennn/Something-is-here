// Instructions

How to play

Step 01
Move through the maze
Navigate dark corridors to find and collect all orbs.
WASDor arrows
Step 02
Collect all orbs
Purple glowing orbs are scattered each level. Grab them all to advance.
Step 03
Hide in darkness
Dark corners conceal you. Lit areas expose you — don't linger in the light.
Step 04
Watch your sanity
Being near the monster drains sanity. Hit zero and it's over even without being touched.
The monster
Three-state AI
Patrol — wanders randomly.
Search — hunts your last position.
Chase — sprints at you. A ! appears above it when it sees you.
Scoring
Points & leaderboard
Each orb scores 10 × your current level. Submit your name after death to appear on the leaderboard.
